## Area C · Pre-consent cookies and trackers

**Rationale**: this is the most heavily sanctioned area and the most easily automatable. The rule is binary: before consent, no non-technical cookie and no call to tracking domains may fire. The regime does not cover only classic cookies, but any storage of or access to information on the user's terminal (localStorage, IndexedDB, invisible pixels, flash cookies, IDFA/Android ID, system identifiers, fingerprinting). The scan must be run with a pristine browser profile, without any interaction with the banner, and repeated across distinct scenarios (pre-consent, post-consent, options management, refusal), comparing what is deposited in each state.

**Legal bases**: Art. 122 Codice Privacy (Italian Privacy Code, D.lgs. 196/2003, implementing ePrivacy) [N2], Art. 5(3) Directive 2002/58/EC (technologically neutral: covers any storage or access on the terminal) [N3], Garante (the Italian DPA) cookie guidelines of 10 June 2021, doc-web 9677876 [G1], EDPB Guidelines 05/2020 on consent [E2], Art. 7 GDPR (demonstrability and withdrawal of consent) [N1], Art. 5(1)(c)(e) GDPR (minimization and storage limitation) [N1], Art. 28 GDPR (controller-processor relationship for third-party tools) [N1]. Methodological and classification support: EDPB Website Auditing Tool [S4, S6], EDPS Website Evidence Collector [S7], CNIL (the French DPA) cookie [S5], Blacklight [S8].

**Client questions (Area C)**

**CMP and proof of consent**
- Which CMP do you use (Iubenda, OneTrust, Cookiebot, custom)? Who configures and maintains it, and when was it last reviewed?
- Where do you store the proofs of consent (CMP cookie, server-side logs, TC String) and for how long? Can you reconstruct, for a single user, date, time, purposes accepted and version of the notice shown?

**Analytics and Consent Mode**
- Do you use Google Consent Mode? Basic or advanced? Who set up the advertising and analytics tags?
- Do you use Google Analytics? In which GDPR configuration (data retention set, Data Processing Amendment signed, IP masking)? Do you do remarketing with Analytics or share the data with other Google products?
- Do you use an audience measurement that you consider exempt from consent (e.g. first-party analytics, self-hosted)? With what configuration (limited data, no cross-referencing with other processing, no sharing with third parties)?

**Third-party pixels and trackers**
- Do you have advertising platform pixels active (Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, Google Ads)? On all pages or only on some (e.g. checkout, thank-you page)? If you use the Meta Pixel, do you have the dedicated notice on every page and the verifiable prior consent required for EU companies?
- Do you use session recording or heatmap tools (Hotjar, Clarity, etc.) that can record interaction and typed fields?

**Connected surfaces: mobile and CRM**
- Do you have a mobile app connected to the site? Does it use third-party SDKs, IDFA/Android ID or geolocation (GPS)? (it is a tracking surface separate from the web)
- Do you have a newsletter, do you do lead generation, or do you have CRM that automatically receive the data collected on the site? With which platforms?

**Tag governance**
- Who has access to the tag manager and is there an approval process before adding a new tag/tracker?
