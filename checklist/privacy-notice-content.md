## Area B · Privacy notice content (Art. 13/14)

**Rationale**: the mere existence of the policy (Area A) is not enough. The privacy notice must contain all the mandatory elements of Articles 13 and 14 GDPR and must correspond to the technical reality of the site. Comparing what the policy declares with what the technical scan detects (third parties, trackers, transfers) is the heart of the audit: discrepancies are the strongest findings, because they prove a breach of the transparency principle and not merely a formal defect. Information must be provided in a concise, transparent, intelligible and easily accessible form, using clear and plain language (Art. 12(1)).

**Legal bases**: Art. 13 GDPR (information for data collected from the data subject), Art. 14 GDPR (indirect collection), Art. 12 GDPR (transparent modalities), Art. 5(1)(a) GDPR (lawfulness, fairness, transparency), Recitals 58-62 [N1]; EDPB Guidelines on Transparency, WP260 rev.01 [E1]. The detailed items rely on the authority checklists [S1, S5] and on the vendor/community checklists used for breadth [S2, S3, S9, S10, S11, S94, S95], never on their own as a foundation.

**Client questions (Area B)**

**Record of processing activities and legal bases**
- Do you have a record of processing activities (Art. 30)? Can we receive an extract to compare it with the published policy? (needed for B-03 and B-13)
- For each purpose, what is the chosen legal basis and where is the reasoning documented? (B-13)
- Who drafted the privacy notice and with what legal support? Is it aligned with the record of processing activities or is it a standalone text?

**Controller and DPO**
- Who is the data controller and which contact details do you want to display in the notice? (B-01)
- Has a DPO been appointed, internal or external? If so, with which public contact details? If not, does one of the mandatory cases under Art. 37 apply (public authority, large-scale monitoring, large-scale special categories)? (B-02)

**Data collected, purposes and profiling**
- Which types of data do you collect from users (name, email, phone, other) and do you list them explicitly in the notice? (B-15)
- For which purposes do you rely on legitimate interest, and have you carried out a Legitimate Interest Assessment (LIA)? (B-04)
- Do you carry out profiling, scoring, lead scoring, automated personalization of content or prices (dynamic pricing)? With what logic and what consequences for the user? (B-10)

**Data collection, recipients and extra-EU transfers**
- Do you have a newsletter? With which platform? Was the list built with verifiable opt-in or imported? (relevant for B-11 and for B-14 if the basis changes)
- Do you do lead generation? Through which channels (site forms, landing pages, lead ads, purchase or enrichment of lists from external suppliers)? (B-11)
- Do you use a CRM or marketing automation (HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, others)? Do the data collected on the site flow into it automatically and are they declared as recipients in the policy? (B-05, B-11)
- Do you have the list of recipients and processors to whom the data are disclosed? Does it match the third parties actually loaded by the site? (B-05, B-12)
- Is the site managed or developed by an external agency? If it accesses the back office, the hosting or receives the form data, have you concluded the processor appointment with an Art. 28 contract? The same applies to CRM, hosting and email marketing platforms. (B-16)
- Where is the site hosted and in which country is the server located? Are there suppliers or infrastructure outside the EU/EEA (hosting, analytics, email, CDN)? Which safeguard do you use for each (adequacy, SCC, BCR, DPF)? (B-06)

**Provision obligations and change management**
- For data collected via forms, which are mandatory by law or by contract and what happens if the user does not provide them? (B-09)
- When you update the notice, do you notify existing users of substantial changes, in particular when purposes or legal bases change? Through which channel (email, in-app notice, banner)? Do you keep a version history of the policy? (B-14, linked to A-08)
